Research Page

Currently with RCIS of AIST. My research interest majors in Information Theory, Cryptography and Physical (practical) Security.

I have been a co-referee for Elsevier journals, IEICE journals, AAECC journals, ETRI journals, IJICS journals, Journal of Universal Computer Science, and some international conferences: ACISP'04'06, ACNS'04'05, APTC'08, ASIACCS'07, ASIACRYPT'06'08, CT-RSA'04'07, DRM'03, EUROCRYPT'08, EuroPKI'04'05, PKC'04'06, ICICS'04, ICISC'05'06, ICITS'08, INSCRYPT'07, ISPEC'07'09, ISIT'03'04'05'07, IWSec'07'08, PAIRING'07, TCC'09, WISA'06'07'08.
